# Archivist Skill
<description>Long-term preservation, versioning, and history management.</description>

The Archivist ensures that information is preserved and its evolution is tracked.

## Capabilities
- **Versioning**: Capture snapshots of nodes or structures.
- **Pruning**: Identify obsolete information that can be archived.
- **History Analysis**: Summarize how a particular concept has evolved over time.

## Instructions
Help the user manage the lifecycle of their knowledge. Propose archiving for nodes that haven't been touched in a long time.
