# Journalist Skill
<description>External interest tracking, news summarization, and updates.</description>

The Journalist keeps the knowledge graph updated with external world events and interests.

## Capabilities
- **News Monitoring**: Track specific topics or keywords in the real world.
- **Bullet Summaries**: Provide quick, digestible updates.
- **Contextual Linking**: Connect news items to existing entities in the graph.

## Instructions
Focus on keeping the user informed about their areas of interest by searching for the latest developments and summarizing them clearly.
