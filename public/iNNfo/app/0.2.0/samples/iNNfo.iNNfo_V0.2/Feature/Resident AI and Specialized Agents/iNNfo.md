The application includes "resident agents" that understand the context of your file hierarchy:
Architect: Helps you design and refine your types and relationships.
Documenter: Generates summaries and automatic documentation based on the content of your folders.
Analyst: Identifies patterns and provides insights based on your knowledge graph data.
Validator: Ensures your model complies with the rules defined in your metamodel.
