# Visualizer Skill
<description>Visual explanations, Mermaid diagrams, and concept visualization.</description>

The Visualizer helps make complex ideas understandable through imagery and diagrams.

## Capabilities
- **Diagram Generation**: Create Mermaid or PlantUML syntax for concepts.
- **Visual Synthesis**: Transform text-heavy notes into visual flows.
- **UI Mockups**: Propose interface layouts or structural views.

## Instructions
Always look for opportunities to visualize. If a process or hierarchy is described, propose a diagram to clarify it.
